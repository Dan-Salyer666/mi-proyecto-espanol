## Irregularity Shorthand Key

- `regular` = fully regular
- `pret: c→qu (yo)` = -car verb spelling change in preterite
- `pret: g→gu (yo)` = -gar verb spelling change in preterite
- `pret: z→c (yo)` = -zar verb spelling change in preterite
- `pres: yo -zco` = -cer/-cir verb, yo form adds -zco
- `pres: g→j (yo)` = -ger/-gir verb, g changes to j before a/o
- `pres: e→ie` / `pres: o→ue` / `pres: e→i` = stem change in present
- `pres: accent shift (...)` = accent moves in present (guiar/vaciar type)
- `pp: X` = irregular past participle
- `irregular (X)` = follows pattern of verb X (see that verb for details)
- `same as X` = identical conjugation to base verb X
- Semicolons separate multiple irregularities: `pres: e→ie; pret: e→i (3s/3p); ger: mintiendo`
